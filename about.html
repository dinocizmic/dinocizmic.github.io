About Me

I was born in Bosnia in the midst of a bloody war. When I was almost three, my family packed our bags and moved to America in search of a better life. A few years later, on a snowy February day, we went to Circuit City and bought our first computer. From that moment on, I was so intruiged by computers, I knew that it was going to be a major part of my future, and that I wanted to study computers in school

Fast forward a decade and here we are, I am working in IT as a Server Administrator, and going to school for computer science. I have been in the IT industry since 2012.

The photo you see in all of my pages was taken in Mostar, Bosnia. This bridge was built in the 16th century. The name of the city comes from the birdge as well, "Most" in Bosnian means bridge.
